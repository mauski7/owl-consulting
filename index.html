About Mauricio "Mau" Arellano

Why did you start Owl Finance & Strategy Consulting?

I am passionate about empowering individuals. With the professional world in perpetual change, entrepreneurship gives entrepreneurs the ability to gain agency and independance. My job is to ensure they have the right tools and insights to succeed.

What differentiated experience do you have that will add value to your clients?

I offer strategic and financial advice to Ohana, the first microfinancing company to drive social and economic development for Mexican families through binational microloans. It offers an alternative to remittances that strengthens communities and promotes financial inclusion.

In my previous life, I was an accomplished equity research and corporate strategy expert specializing in transforming insights into actionable investment theses. My professional journey is underpinned by nearly a decade of experience, during which I honed the intricacies of performing financial analyses, executing equity valuations, and developing corporate strategies.
